Company Overview

ByteBite delivers fresh, chef-prepared meal prep boxes to busy professionals in Frankfurt, Cologne, and Düsseldorf. Subscribers choose from four plans (Classic, Keto, Vegan, High-Protein) and receive 3 or 5 delivery days per week. All meals are portioned, labelled with macros, and require zero preparation.

Founding Story

Origin

Raj Sharma cooked in hotel kitchens for eight years before growing tired of wasted food and one-size-fits-all catering. Sofia Klein had run last-mile logistics for a pharmaceutical company and knew exactly how to make cold-chain delivery reliable. They tested their first 20 boxes with colleagues in 2022. Within three months they had 80 subscribers. Within a year, 1,100.

Products & Services

  • Classic Plan — balanced meals, Mediterranean-inspired, 2,000 kcal/day
  • Keto Plan — high-fat, low-carb; 5% carbs, 70% fat, 25% protein
  • Vegan Plan — 100% plant-based, fortified with B12 and iron
  • High-Protein Plan — 160g+ protein/day for athletes and gym-goers
  • 3-Day Pack (Mon/Wed/Fri) and 5-Day Pack (weekday delivery)

Market & Competitive Landscape

The meal-kit and meal prep market in the DACH region is valued at €4.5 billion and growing at 11% annually. HelloFresh dominates the kit segment; ByteBite competes on the fully-prepared (no cooking required) segment — which lacks a clear market leader.

Competitor Position
HelloFresh Meal kits (cooking required), stronger brand, larger scale
Foodspring Protein-focused, supplement crossover audience
Lieferando partner kitchens Convenience, no meal plan structure
Local meal prep businesses Highly local, inconsistent quality

Business Challenge

⚠️ Current Challenge

ByteBite's customer acquisition cost (€45) versus lifetime value (€180) gives a payback period of roughly 4 months — manageable, but not if churn stays above 6% per month. The team has 6 months of raw order data but no clean dataset and no KPI dashboard.

Your Project Brief

As a team, you will work with this company throughout the 6-day course. Each day builds on the last. Below is a summary of the four deliverables that form your final submission on Day 7.
