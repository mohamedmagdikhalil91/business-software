Company Overview

WordWise Global is a multilingual content marketing agency specialising in SEO-driven content, brand storytelling, and social media strategy. They serve 24 retainer clients across Austria, Germany, and Switzerland, producing long-form articles, email newsletters, LinkedIn content, and brand voice documentation in up to six languages.

Founding Story

Origin

Elena Rodriguez had been a tech journalist and SEO writer for eight years before realising that the clients paying agencies €80,000 a year for content were getting generic copy written by junior staff. Kai Hoffmann had just left Google Vienna after six years managing advertiser accounts. They founded WordWise Global in 2019 with one rule: every piece of content must measurably serve the client's business goal — or they would rewrite it for free.

Products & Services

  • SEO Content Packages — keyword research, monthly articles, performance tracking
  • Social Media Management — LinkedIn, Instagram, XING; strategy + production + scheduling
  • Newsletter Production — design, copy, send, A/B testing, analytics report
  • Brand Voice Documentation — tone of voice guide, messaging frameworks, editorial guidelines
  • Translation & Localisation — content adaptation for DE/AT/CH/FR/ES/EN markets

Market & Competitive Landscape

The global digital marketing and content industry is valued at over €400 billion. Within the DACH region, content marketing spend by SMEs and scale-ups is growing at 14% annually as organic search becomes increasingly competitive.

Business Challenge

⚠️ Current Challenge

WordWise Global has eight people producing content for 24 clients — with no standardised reporting template. Every account manager builds their monthly performance report from scratch in a different format. Clients are complaining about inconsistent reporting, and new-client onboarding takes 3 weeks because there are no templates.

Your Project Brief

As a team, you will work with this company throughout the 6-day course. Each day builds on the last. Below is a summary of the four deliverables that form your final submission on Day 7.

Task Deliverable / Description Tool
Build a standardised client report template Monthly performance report: branded Word document, 4 sections (summary, SEO, social, newsletter) Word
Create a client KPI tracking dashboard Traffic, rankings, engagement, revenue attribution — Excel, one sheet per client Excel
Build a pitch deck for new clients 6-slide deck: Agency intro, services, case studies, process, pricing, next steps PowerPoint
Clean and analyse the campaign performance data Fix raw data, compute avg traffic lift, rank clients by performance Excel
