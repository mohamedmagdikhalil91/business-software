Company Overview

Green Threads designs and sells GOTS-certified organic streetwear: hoodies, T-shirts, and caps made from upcycled and certified-organic materials. Every product carries a QR code linking to its supply chain — from cotton farm to finished garment. The brand sells directly to consumers online and through four retail partners.

Founding Story

Origin

Arjun Mehta graduated from ArtEZ Fashion Design in 2019 with a deep frustration: the fashion industry he loved was one of the world's most polluting. Lena Baumann had been consulting for sustainability certification bodies and knew exactly how to navigate organic supply chains. Together they decided to build a brand that proved sustainable fashion could be profitable — and genuinely cool. Their first viral TikTok generated 2,400 orders in 48 hours.

Products & Services

  • Organic Cotton T-Shirts — 190g GOTS-certified, 8 colourways, unisex sizes XS–3XL
  • Upcycled Hoodies — made from post-consumer textile waste, limited seasonal drops
  • Organic Caps — 5-panel + dad cap styles, embroidered logo
  • Bundle Packs — T-shirt + Hoodie + tote bag (seasonal, limited edition)
  • B2B Branded Merchandise — corporate clients wanting sustainable branded apparel

Market & Competitive Landscape

The sustainable fashion market is growing at 9.7% annually and is projected to reach €65 billion by 2030. Gen Z and Millennial consumers are increasingly willing to pay a premium for traceable, ethical products.

Business Challenge

⚠️ Current Challenge

Green Threads manages inventory across three spreadsheets that were never designed to talk to each other. Bestselling hoodies sell out regularly, while slow-moving T-shirt colourways tie up capital in the warehouse. Last quarter, 23 stock-out incidents led to an estimated €18,000 in lost sales.
