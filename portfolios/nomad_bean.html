Company Overview

Nomad Bean is a specialty coffee subscription brand that sources single-origin beans directly from small farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. Each monthly box includes freshly roasted beans, an origin story card, and tasting notes — turning morning coffee into a ritual worth talking about.

Founding Story

Origin

Mia Chen spent six years as a regional manager at a global coffee chain, watching premium coffee turned into a commodity. Tobias Rau, a specialty coffee importer, had the supply side: direct relationships with 12 farms across three continents. In 2022 they combined their networks to launch Nomad Bean — a subscription that cuts out the middleman and lets customers taste coffee the way farmers intended it.

Products & Services

  • Monthly Subscription Box (Explorer €14.99 / Connoisseur €22.99 / Roaster's Choice €34.99)
  • Corporate Coffee Sourcing — bulk direct-trade supply for offices and hotel groups
  • Pop-up Tasting Events — ticketed cupping sessions at partner venues
  • Gift Subscriptions — 3-month or 6-month prepaid plans

Market & Competitive Landscape

The European specialty coffee market is valued at €4.2 billion and growing at 8% per year. Consumer premiumisation and a growing interest in sustainability and provenance are tailwinds.

Business Challenge

⚠️ Current Challenge

Nomad Bean is losing roughly 34 subscribers every month (8% churn). Customers cite monotonous selections and lack of a loyalty experience. Mia and Tobias need a data-driven retention strategy, a professional investor pitch deck for a planned €150K seed raise, and a Word-formatted proposal template to pursue three corporate accounts.
