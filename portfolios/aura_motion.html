Company Overview

Aura Motion is a boutique motion graphics and video production agency serving tech scale-ups, D2C brands, and non-profits. They produce brand animations, explainer videos, and social media content that combine aesthetic precision with measurable engagement outcomes.

Founding Story

Origin

Priya Kapoor left Publicis after realising that great motion design was being buried under agency bureaucracy and bloated timelines. Sam Adler had been directing independent films and felt the same frustration from the creative side. They launched Aura Motion to be the studio they always wished existed — fast, collaborative, and obsessed with craft. Their first client was a Berlin fintech; within a year they had a 3-person team and a waiting list.

Products & Services

  • Brand Animation Packages — logo reveals, brand style guides brought to life
  • Explainer Videos — 60–90 second animated product/service explainers
  • Social Media Content Packages — monthly retainer, 8–12 short-form videos
  • Event Highlight Reels — post-event edited films (same-day turnaround)
  • Corporate Training Videos — L&D content for internal use

Market & Competitive Landscape

The global video marketing industry is valued at €12 billion and growing at 15% annually. Short-form video consumption is at an all-time high, and brands are under pressure to produce professional animated content at speed.

Business Challenge

⚠️ Current Challenge

Aura Motion has a recurring project management problem: timelines slip, client feedback loops go untracked, and the founders have no single view of what is in progress. Three projects have missed deadlines this quarter, one client nearly churned. They need a structured task management setup, a standardised project tracker, and a professional proposal template.
