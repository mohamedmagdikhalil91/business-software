The Narrative

The story that ties all 6 days together

📖 Your Story

You and your co-founder just launched a micro-business. Two of you. A great idea, a motivated team, and zero infrastructure. No templates. No processes. No professional documents.

Over the next 6 days, your team will build everything from scratch: brand identity, client communications, a financial model, a task management system — and finally, a pitch to convince an investor that your business is worth backing.

By Day 7, you won't just know how to use these tools — you'll have used them to build something real together.

This course does not try to teach you everything. It teaches you the 20% of skills that will handle 80% of your daily work as a business professional. No SQL, no VBA, no complexity for its own sake.

Choose Your Company

Pick one startup to work with for the entire course. Your project deliverables will be built around this business.

📌
How to choose Read each profile carefully and decide as a team. Both team members will work on the same company for all 6 days — your project deliverables build the complete digital infrastructure for that business.

☕ Food & Beverage

A specialty coffee subscription brand sourcing single-origin beans directly from small farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. Sells D2C via subscription boxes and B2B to corporate clients.

📊 Your dataset: Subscription order export — dates, plans, pricing, statuses, and customer data to clean and analyse.

Aura Motion

"Motion design that makes brands impossible to ignore."
🎨 Creative Services

A boutique motion graphics and video production agency serving tech scale-ups and D2C brands. Produces brand animations, explainer videos, and social media content.

📊 Your dataset: Client project tracker — budgets, timelines, actual delivery dates, and hours logged per project.

Green Threads

"Streetwear that wears its values on its sleeve."
🏭 Sustainable Fashion

A GOTS-certified organic streetwear brand producing hoodies, tees, and caps from upcycled materials. Sells direct-to-consumer online and through four retail partners.

📊 Your dataset: Sales & inventory export — SKUs, sizes, colours, order values, and channel data with formatting issues.

Zenith Wellness

"Mind, body, and business — find your peak."

A premium yoga and pilates studio in Munich with a growing online platform. Corporate wellness packages are a key revenue stream alongside in-studio memberships.

📊 Your dataset: Session booking log — class types, instructors, attendance flags, and plan data transcribed from WhatsApp.

ByteBite

"Healthy meal prep, perfectly portioned, delivered fresh."
📦 FoodTech · D2C

A meal prep delivery service for busy professionals in Frankfurt, Cologne, and Düsseldorf. Four plans (Classic, Keto, Vegan, High-Protein) with 3 or 5 delivery days.

📊 Your dataset: 6-month order export — customer names, plans, delivery dates, box prices, statuses, and cities.

WordWise Global

A multilingual content marketing agency with 24 retainer clients across the DACH region. Produces SEO articles, newsletters, and social media content in up to 6 languages.

Urban Canopy

"AI-powered urban growing — from seed to harvest, guided."
